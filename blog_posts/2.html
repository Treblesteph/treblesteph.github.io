gravitas part I: knowledge

14th january 2016

In this post I will outline my plans for producing some useful tools, and furthering my knowledge as described in my first post. I will approach these projects as mini sprints, where I work intensively for two or three weeks at a time. I hope that I can produce a basic version of each one during the two weeks, but if not, I can prolong the work for an additional week, or dedicate another two weeks again in the future. I can also revisit projects in future sprints to add features. The following plan will be subject to vast changes as I develop my skills and ideas.
